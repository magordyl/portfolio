## Thesis

Here's what students and early-career people should do to protect themselves. Specifics: Use AI to accelerate learning, not replace it. Why - to be an effective operator of LLMs, you need to know when they're going wrong. AI provides amazing potential to accelerate learning so you can do this with (eg) 2 years of experience instead of 10. In practice: don't just give tasks to an LLM and accept the output.
