Tighter list, familiar pattern. Each row is minimal. Tap opens the SnoozeDrawer already built in 4.2 (Reschedule / Done / Archive options). Scales to 20+ overdue items cleanly. Trade-off: one extra tap per item, and the user's attention briefly leaves the step (drawer is modal-like).

How to decide

All options respect the locked Forest × Spring palette, Inter/Inter Tight typography, Balanced density, and the dual-accent rule. None introduces a new font or breaks the design system. Trade-offs are size of the surface (Q2, Q4), semantic colour cost (Q3), and flow friction (Q5). Pick one per question; answers feed back into sub-commit-4-3-weekly-review-design.md §9.
